Day 2 – December 1st

08:30 – 12:30   Clear management and leadership with impact

Effective leadership for post-docs (Vincent Hendriks, ARDIS management consultancy). This course cover topics of high relevance to early- and mid-career researchers:

·      Coaching and motivating subordinates.

·      Providing constructive feedback.

·      Making clear agreements.

·      Managing downwards, upwards, and sideways.

During this session breaks are scheduled by the course holder.

12:30 – 13:30   Lunch

Catered lunch at the conference venue.

13:30 – 16:30   Effective presentations

Effective presentations training (Karin Herrebout, Moile Moile). This is an interactive session. The session will focus on areas not normally covered including:

·      Clear voice use and voice projection.

·      Use of non-verbal communications while presenting.

·      Make your story heard, seen, and felt.

During this session breaks are scheduled by the course holder.
